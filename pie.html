PIE - Blockchain Charity Platform


Role

User Experience Designer

Hackathon

Hack the North 2018

Duration

36 Hours


Brief

PIE is a platform that uses the capabilities of blockchain in order to make secure cryptocurrency-based donations to charities. The platform keeps charities accountable for their transactions through ledgers. We wanted to empower users on the platform to donate to causes they care about, with the ability to vote on proposals or causes that matter to them.



User Story

As a: Charity Donor
I want to: track what my donation is being used towards
So that: I can confirm that my money is used towards the cause I am passionate about and keep charities accountable

Site designed and coded by me!
