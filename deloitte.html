Summary

I was a user experience designer on a design team consisting of 5 UX designers and 2 UI designers. The platform we were building had a large user base, and the design decisions we made would impact the experience of many.

My day-to-day involves quickly iterating and wireframing for several user stories while we work in an agile process. I am normally responsible for creating the experience for several stories within a sprint, in web, responsive web, iOS and android. While designing, I also ensure that all tech and business requirements are met while advocating for the user. I supported my design decisions with best practices and existing user data, constant feedback, and through building out user task flow charts.

My team and I often interacted with developers, the delivery team, and stakeholders in order to ensure the success of deliverables and the project.
